FRAME THE NARRATIVE: "Residents accuse Council and US tech firm of corrupting democratic survey."

▶ LEVERAGE POLITICAL REPRESENTATIVES

BRIEF THE LOCAL MP

Formally brief the local Member of Parliament (MP). Provide them with the entire evidence package. An MP can raise the issue in Parliament, write directly to government ministers, and put formal pressure on STC.

MOBILIZE OPPOSITION COUNCILLORS

Identify and brief opposition councillors within STC. Provide them with the evidence and questions to ask in full council meetings, forcing the issue onto the public record.

▶ MOBILISE THE COMMUNITY

Hold a public meeting. Present the findings, explain how the process was compromised, and launch a petition demanding:

  • The survey be quashed
  • Re-run fairly
  • Paid for by Granicus
